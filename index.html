Clear Faith Ministries

The mission/vision of Clear Faith Christian Ministries is to serve the body of Christ through Prayer, Teaching, Fellowship and Services. We believe that we were created to serve the Kingdom and expand it one soul at a time.

This online ministry focuses on reaching those online who have lost hope in the traditional church yet still have faith in God through Jesus Christ. Our aim is to provide fervent prayer, relevant study, true and meaningful fellowship, and integral services to the least, the left out, and the lost in and outside the church

Today, Clear Faith Christian Ministries continues to teach on Facebook and has added other ministries:

- The 52-week Savings Challenge: An online savings program that encourages the members and those following the ministry to save.

- Midnight Chronicles: A broadcast at midnight where the Pastor shares thought related to life and ministry.
(During the summer: June-August)

- The Biblical Discussions Broadcast: The channel that hosts teachings from books and bible studies on Facebook Live.
(Thursdays and Sundays)


Our outreach ministry is the Clear Faith Christian Counseling, Consulting, and Coaching Services.

- Counseling: Marital, Relationship,Family
- Consulting: Business, Church
- Coaching: Life, Career
